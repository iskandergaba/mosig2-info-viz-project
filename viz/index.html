CO2 Emissions by Destinations Continents

This Visualization gives a global insight on how the CO2 emissions are distributed across continents by year.
It also compares continent distinations emissions in order to find out the continent destinantion most responsible of CO2.We have two quantitativevariables (time(year), CO2 value), thatâ€™s why Ichoose to represent my data in two axis x and y. By convention, Iput time in the x axis as a good practice.The use of the color and the text to represent the continent is very important to help the user to see the differenceof CO2 emissionsbetween continentand the greatestvalueof CO2.Without forgetting, that the number of continentsis 5which give us the possibility to use colors to make difference.
